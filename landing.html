SCIENCE

By pursuing the Science stream, you can either opt for Biology, Physics, and Chemistry [BiPC subjects] and pave a way for starting a career in the medical field or you can study Maths, Physics, and Chemistry [MPC subjects] to pursue courses apart from Medical Science like Engineering, Bachelor of Arts, Bachelor of Science, Architecture, Robotics, etc.

COMMERCE

Commerce deals with the various aspects of business, trade, accounting, financial information/transactions and merchandising.

ARTS

Arts subjects include Economics, History, Political Science, Geography, Sociology, Philosophy, Psychology, Computer Science, Hindi, Regional Language, etc. There are plenty of careers after the Arts stream, which ranges from Journalism and Literary Studies to Law and Hotel Management.
